NORTH SUMATRA: DIVE SITES, MOUNTAINS AND TRIBAL CULTURE

Crystal-clear seabeds, tribal culture flourishing, mountains framing emerald valleys. Northern Sumatra, rich in diversity.

≈ 31
Days
472

📖 17 min read 🔄 Last updated: 2026-01-10

Discover Banda Aceh between spiritual fervor and rebuilt memory, where mosques and streets tell of deep resilience. Reach Pulau Weh to dive into clear waters with living reefs, then find the coolness of Berastagi between volcanoes and fruit orchards. Bukittinggi affirms Minangkabau culture through its traditional houses and lively markets.

  • Pulau Weh: world-class diving
  • Berastagi: highland fruit farms
  • Bukittinggi: minangkabau culture
  • Harau Valley: gorge trekking

❓ Frequently asked questions

What weather should you expect?

Dry Season (May-September): Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal temperatures, clear skies, peak tourist season, best diving conditions, advance booking recommended.

Shoulder Season (April & October): Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate rainfall, reasonable prices, excellent conditions, transitional seasons, good value.

Wet Season (November-March): Heavy rainfall, lush landscapes, tropical storms, humidity high, lowest prices, fewer tourists, challenging conditions.

Regional Variations: Diverse climates, equatorial zones, monsoon patterns, altitude differences, island variations, weather monitoring essential, seasonal planning.

How many days should I plan?

We believe you will enjoy this itinerary best by dedicating between 25 and 37 days. The next step will allow you to adjust the duration of your stay.

What to discover in Indonesia?

World's largest archipelago with incredible diversity, pristine beaches, and rich cultural heritage. Experience Bali's temples, Java's volcanoes, and authentic Indonesian culture.
