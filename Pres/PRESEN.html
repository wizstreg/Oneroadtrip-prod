1What is OneRoadTrip?

OneRoadTrip is a free travel itinerary platform. It allows users to browse, customize, and create complete itineraries with interactive maps, route calculations, detailed descriptions for every stop, and hotel integration.

Contrairement aux outils qui partent d'une page blanche (Wanderlog, TripIt…), ORT propose un catalogue de 640+ itinéraires prêts à l'emploi couvrant 90 pays et 4 500+ points of interest, disponibles en 5 langues complètes (français, anglais, espagnol, italien, portugais) avec une 6e en cours (arabe).

2How does it work?

For travelers

  • Browse the itinerary catalog (as a list or on a map) by country, region, or theme
  • View each stop with detailed visits, photos, and practical tips
  • Customize the itinerary: add, remove, or reorder stops and visits
  • View everything on an interactive map with distance and time calculations
  • Book hotels, flights, cars, etc. directly from the itinerary
  • Import bookings into your saved trip
  • Export as a travel journal, share with family and friends
  • Access your trip offline
  • And many more features

4 ways to create an itinerary

From the catalog or on a map

Choose from 640+ ready-to-use itineraries, browsable by country, region, or theme.

From scratch

Build an itinerary step by step from 4,500+ listed places with visits, or by inserting a new unlisted place.

Web page import

Transform a blog post or magazine article into an interactive itinerary — with no AI hallucination.

By departure point

Select a departure or arrival point to generate a tailored itinerary.

3Key features

Editorial content

Every stop contains hand-crafted editorial content: things to see, activities, practical tips, recommended duration. Travel guide content, not a Google card.

Carbon score

Every itinerary displays a carbon score evaluating its ecological impact based on distances, transport mode, and number of stops.

Multilingual

Interface and itineraries in 5 full languages (FR, EN, ES, IT, PT). The same itinerary accessible to an international audience.

Artificial intelligence

Web import, mobile AI guide (photo → place narrated), image translation, AI-generated detailed place reviews.
