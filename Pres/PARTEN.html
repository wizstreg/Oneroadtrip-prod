1OneRoadTrip at a glance

OneRoadTrip is a free itinerary-building platform. Unlike tools that start from a blank page, ORT offers a catalog of 640+ ready-to-use itineraries across 90 countries, which users can browse, customize, or recreate from scratch. Every itinerary is available in 5 full languages (French, English, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese) with a 6th in progress (Arabic).

The platform features an interactive map with routing, detailed descriptions for each stop (visits, activities, practical tips), hotel integration, a carbon score evaluating each road trip's ecological impact, and advanced AI features (itinerary generation, web page import, photo recognition).

2What only OneRoadTrip does

The trip planner market is crowded. Wanderlog, Roadtrippers, TripIt, Layla, Komoot, TripPlanner AI… Noe combines all of the following. Here are OneRoadTrip's 8 exclusive features:

1

Ready-to-use itineraries in 5 languages

No competitor offers a catalog of complete, detailed itineraries available in 5 languages. ORT offers 640+ itineraries you can browse immediately, each with detailed visits, practical tips, and photos.

2

Carbon score built into every itinerary

No trip planner on the market includes a carbon score. ORT calculates the ecological footprint of each road trip based on distances, transport mode, and number of stops.

3

Detailed editorial visits for every stop

On ORT, every stop contains editorial text: things to see, suggested activities, practical tips, recommended duration. Travel guide content, not a directory listing.

4

Web page import → itinerary without hallucination

ORT transforms any web page into an interactive itinerary with map, routing, and hotels in minutes. Unlike generative AI tools, ORT relies exclusively on the source page's existing content.

5

Mobile AI guide: photo → place narrated or translated

While traveling, the user takes a photo of a monument, landscape, or sign. The AI (Gemini) identifies the place and tells its story, or translates the text in the image.

6

Automatic nights-per-destination calculation

ORT calcule automatiquement le nombre optimal de nuits par destination en fonction des distances et du nombre de points of interest. Les concurrents laissent ce travail à l'utilisateur.

7

White-label and B2B integration

ORT is designed to be embedded into partner platforms: tourism boards, publishers, booking platforms. The tool can be deployed as a white-label solution with the partner's content and branding.

8

SEO static pages per itinerary

Each itinerary generates a Google-indexable static HTML page, SEO-optimized with meta descriptions, structured titles, and rich data.

  • Your international visitors access itineraries in their language
  • Carbon score showcasing your CSR commitment
  • Free during pilot phase, then SaaS or commission model

✈️ Travel creators & influencers

  • Publish your itineraries on ORT with a backlink to your site
  • Benefit from ORT's traffic and SEO ranking in 5 languages
  • Your itineraries presented with your name and branding
  • Win-win model: traffic exchange, zero cost

📰 Publishers & travel media

  • Your "Top 10" articles and guides become interactive itineraries
  • Automatic import of your existing web pages
  • New revenue stream through hotel affiliation
  • Increased time-on-page and user engagement

🏨 Booking platforms

  • A guest books a cottage in the Ardennes → ORT suggests a 3-day road trip
  • Increased length of stay and average booking value
  • Lightweight technical integration (links, widget, API)
  • Model: revenue share on generated bookings

5Technical architecture

ORT runs on a static, resilient architecture. Itineraries are served as pre-generated JSON files via Netlify Pro (global CDN). The marginal cost of an additional visitor is near zero — a spike of 50,000 visitors in a few hours is absorbed with no significant additional cost.
